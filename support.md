## Common topics

### Purchases and power-ups

Purchases are handled by **Apple App Store** billing. For payment issues, refunds, or receipt problems, you may also use Apple’s purchase history and support flows.

### Game Center and leaderboards (iOS)

Leaderboards require an Apple ID signed into Game Center. If scores do not appear, verify Game Center is enabled in iOS Settings and that you are on a supported build (not all preview builds include Game Center).

### Lost progress

Gameplay data is stored **on your device**. Uninstalling the app or clearing data may erase progress and inventory. We cannot restore purely local data without a cloud account feature.

### Controls

You can switch between buttons, tilt, and tap-side controls in **Settings**.
