**Contact:** orikal3107@gmail.com

## 1. Summary

FrogClimb is primarily an **offline game**. We **do not operate our own game servers** and **do not maintain user accounts inside the App** for login purposes. Most gameplay data (such as scores, preferences, and in-game inventory) is stored **locally on your device** using the device’s storage (for example via React Native Async Storage).

Certain optional features rely on **Apple** or **Google** platform services (for example **in-app purchases** and, on supported iOS builds, **Apple Game Center**). Those companies process information under their own policies.

---

## 2. Information the App processes on your device (local storage)

The App may store the following **on your device** to provide functionality:

- **Gameplay and progression:** best height / score, star or collectible counters, and related gameplay state  
- **Shop and power-ups:** inventory counts, free-use counters, and timestamps used for daily reward eligibility  
- **Settings:** control scheme (buttons / tilt / tap sides), language, audio and vibration preferences  
- **Cosmetics:** selected character and accessory choices  

This locally stored information is **not automatically transmitted to us** by the App as currently designed. If you uninstall the App or clear its data, this information may be deleted depending on your operating system.

---

## 3. Device sensors (tilt controls)

If you enable **tilt** controls, the App reads **device motion / accelerometer** data **on the device** to move the character. This motion data is used **only for gameplay input** in real time and **is not used for advertising** and **is not sent to our servers** (we do not operate an analytics backend for this App as currently released).

---

## 4. Audio

The App may play **sound effects and music** through standard device audio APIs. The App **does not record** your microphone.

---

## 5. In-app purchases (Apple App Store / Google Play)

Purchases are processed by **Apple** and/or **Google** through their store billing systems. We do **not** receive your full payment card number.

We may receive limited purchase-related information through the store platform necessary to deliver consumable items (for example product identifiers and transaction status on the device). For details on how Apple or Google handles payment and purchase information, see:

- Apple: [https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)  
- Google: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
